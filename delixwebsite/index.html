Empowering Learning and Interactive eXperiences

The elixLab, led by Dr. Parmit Chilana and affiliated with SFU’s ixLab, studies how people interact with intelligent technologies. We design human-centered AI tools that support learning, creativity, and everyday work, aiming to make emerging technologies more understandable, usable, and empowering.
